Who We Are

A Brokerage Built on Experience & Integrity

Rallys Equities (Pvt) Ltd is a licensed brokerage firm operating on the Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX), founded in 2025 by a father-and-son leadership team with more than 35 years of combined experience. The company holds a valid Trading Right Entitlement Certificate (TREC No. 553), authorizing it to provide brokerage services in full compliance with PSX and SECP regulations. From its inception, Rallys Equities has focused on building a robust operational framework supported by secure settlement and custody mechanisms, strong internal controls, and a client-first service philosophy.

Our services are designed to support a diverse client base — including individual investors, corporates, and institutions — through structured access to equities, funds, fixed-income instruments, and Shariah-compliant investment solutions.

With transparent reporting, reliable trade execution, and dedicated client support, Rallys Equities ensures that every transaction is handled with accuracy and accountability. Understand the Basics

Top Five Investment Strategies
1. Long-Term Value Investing
Focus on buying fundamentally strong companies listed on PSX and holding them for long-term growth — analyze fundamentals rather than reacting to market swings.
2. Dividend Investing
Choose companies that provide consistent dividend payouts — stable firms that deliver recurring income to shareholders.
3. Growth Stock Investing
Invest in emerging companies and high-growth sectors such as technology, banking, and energy — higher returns with greater volatility risk.
4. Diversification Strategy
Spread your investments across different sectors, asset classes, and companies listed on PSX, so underperformance in one holding is offset by gains elsewhere.
5. Short-Term Trading
Benefit from price fluctuations within days or weeks using technical analysis and market timing — demands considerable expertise.
Beginner Tips
Diversify across sectors · Avoid emotional decisions · Invest within your means · Prioritize long-term growth · Consult licensed professionals.

CAGR Calculator
Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) represents the average yearly growth rate of your investment over a specific period — it smooths out the returns and shows the average annual growth rate.
CAGR = (Ending Value ÷ Beginning Value)^(1/N) − 1

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about PSX investing, accounts, platforms, and support.

What is the Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX)?
The Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX) is the premier marketplace where investors buy and sell shares of listed companies. It provides a regulated and transparent platform for trading stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and other securities.
How do I open a trading account with your brokerage firm?
Complete the account opening form, submit identification and financial documents, and receive your login credentials after verification. You can also apply online via the official PSX eClear portal.
What services do you provide as a stock brokerage firm?
Stock trading and execution, market research and investment insights, portfolio management, corporate advisory services, customer support and investor education.
Is my investment safe with your brokerage?
Yes. We are a licensed and SECP-regulated stock brokerage firm in PSX. Client investments are managed under strict compliance with SECP and PSX rules, ensuring security, transparency, and accountability.
What is the minimum investment required to start trading in PSX?
There is no fixed minimum investment set by PSX. However, your investment depends on the share prices of the companies you choose.
What trading platforms do you offer?
Modern platforms enabling real-time stock trading, market data access, portfolio monitoring, and order placement via mobile or desktop applications.
Do you provide investment advice?
Yes. Our research analysts and relationship managers provide investment guidance, stock recommendations, and market insights to help clients make informed decisions.
How do I withdraw funds from my trading account?
Submit withdrawal requests through the platform or customer support for direct bank transfer processing.
What are your brokerage charges?
Competitive rates with transparent pricing and no hidden charges; detailed fee schedules are available upon request.
